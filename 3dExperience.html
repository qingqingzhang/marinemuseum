Tutorial

This is the tutorial created for the Desktop interactive exhibits of the H. Lee White Maritime Museum in Oswego.

The Oswego LightHouse

The Oswego Harbor West Pierhead Lighthouse was built in 1934 to replace a previous lighthouse built in 1880. The iconic building has been recreated here, documented with historical information from the H. Lee White Maritime Museum.

Major Lundie's Schoolhouse

A depiction of Fort Ontario in the 18th century; an important fort for keeping important waterways under British control. This scene is based on George Gray’s “Major Lundie’s School House on Wheels”. The Schoolhouse, Anne McVickar, and Basil Dunbar's duel are featured in this scene.

The Treaty of Oswego

A depiction of the Treaty of Oswego, a George Gray mural. In this scene, Chief Pontiac, an Ottawa War Chief, can be seen negotiating with Robert Rogers. Featured in this scene are Pontiac, Rogers, trade goods, The Northwest Passage, and Roger's Rangers.
