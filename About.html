About the Project

Lake Ontario Twilight by James Lloyd

The H. Lee White Maritime Museum is a nonprofit dedicated to educating the community on the history associated with the Oswego River and its drainage basin, the Oswego Canal and Lake Ontario. Through the development of the Maritime A.R. project, we aim to leverage augmented and virtual reality technology to further this message and increase accessibility to educational materials.
 
The Maritime A.R. project provides both an enhanced in person museum experience, along with providing an immersive exhibit experience for those visiting virtually.
 
This project is a joint effort between the Human-computer Interaction program at SUNY Oswego and the H. Lee White Maritime Museum sponsored by the Richard Shineman Foundation.
