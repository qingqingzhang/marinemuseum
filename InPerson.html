Lighthouse at Sunset by Patrick Kinane

Download the Artivive Application

Open the photo app on your phone, then scan the QR code provided by the museum. This will prompt you to download Artivive from your app store.

Find a Compass

Any exhibits with this icon have added interactive features that may be viewed using the Artivive app.

Point your Phone at the Exhibit

Open the Artivive app and scan the painting or model to learn more about Oswego’s maritime history. Audio narration will play automatically with headphones plugged into your device.
